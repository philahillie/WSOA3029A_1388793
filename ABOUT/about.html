RATIONALE

This website aims to analyse trends in the film and television world by providing data surrounding the ratings, the audiences and also (hopefully) through investigating the possible impact the COVID-19 pandemic has had on ratings and earnings of films and television shows.

Film and television are visual mediums and thus I thought communicating its data thorugh the use of data visualisation would be pair up nicely. Usually, statistics surrounding film and television are more descriptive so I wanted to use this opportunity to create concise and easy to read summaries or audiences and film lovers so they could feel part of the whole film experience.
