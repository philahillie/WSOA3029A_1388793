WOMEN'S RATING OF GoT

In my first two data visualisations, I chose to look at the ratings of a show called "Game of Thrones". Through the use of the Internet Movie Database (IMDb) web application programming interface (API), I was able to get insight into how men and women of different age groups rated the television show. Overall, the series got an aggregate rating of 9.2 out of 10. The age groups I looked at were people younger than 18 years old, men and women aged between 18 and 29 years old, 30 and 44 years old and men and women older than 45 years old. Generally, the ratings across both genders and most of the age groups were consistent and ranged between 9 and 10 out 10. The only group that was slightly different were women aged under 18 years old who rated the show an average of 7.4 out of 10.

I wanted to use this information to look into how the show is received by men and women. I can imagine that younger, teenage women would not enjoy the show as much because of the violence and sexualisation of women that is portrayed throughout the series. I initially wanted to make use of a line graph however, the use of a scatter plot still communicated the values whilst keeping the graphics simple. I also considered adding lines in the background of the graphs to help the viewers align the plotted circles with the numerical values they were depicted but decided to keep the graph simple.
